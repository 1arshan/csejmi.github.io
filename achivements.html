ACHIVEMENTS

Video Lecture Series by Mr. Sarfaraz Masood.

Mr. Sarfaraz Masood, Assistant Professor in the department of Computer Engineering has enthusiastically produced a series of video lectures on Compiler Design. The video lectures cover a wide range of that potentially include Lexical Analysis, Syntax Analysis, Semantic Analysis and Intermediate Code Generation

Automatic Student Attendance Mobile App by Mr. Danish Raza Rizvi and Shivaank Tripathy

Shivaank Tripathy, the 4th year Computer Engineering student under the guidance of Mr. Danish Raza Rizvi have developed a Mobile Application that registers attendances of students automatically , almost without any human involvement. It allows attendance to be taken in mere seconds, centralizes the attendance database and eliminates the usage of paper altogether.

Geo-Fencing Box by Mr. M Zeeshan Ansari and Abhinav Rai

Abhinav Rai, 4th Year Computer Engineering and his team under the Mentorship of their faculty members Mr. M Zeeshan Ansari and Mr. Faiyaz Ahmad, proposed an outstanding solution for the problem of ever-increasing UAVs (Unmanned Aerial Vehicles) that may cause troubles in air traffic. This problem was given in the Smart India Hackathon - 2017 by the Ministry of Civil Aviation. The team was all India winner at Smart India Hackathon – 2017 and was awarded prize of 1 Lakh Rupees.

Mr. M Zeeshan Ansari & Mohammad Musharraf’s Smart Home Appliance Control

Mohammad Musharraf along with his team of Rashid, Amish, Shafaat, Shubham and Ariba under the mentorship of Mr. M Zeeshan Ansari delivered an innovative project for automating the operation of Home Appliances through Mobile App. This app can be integrated with and used to control and monitor all appliances that are present at home.

Automated Essay Grading by Prof. Tanvir Ahmad & Ishita Tripathy

Prof. Tanvir of the computer engineering department and his student Ishita designed an Automated Essay Grading System. The purpose of this project is to implement and train natural language processing algorithms to asses and grade essay responses automatically. The final system performs better than state-of-the-art systems on 3 out of 8 sets in the database.
